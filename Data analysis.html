Nokia

Nokia is a Finnish multinational telecommunications equipment, information technology, and consumer electronics corporation. Originally founded as a paper mill in 1865, Nokia has evolved significantly throughout its history. Today, the company is a major player in the global telecommunications industry, specializing in network infrastructure solutions such as 5G, 4G, and optical networks. Nokia offers a diverse range of products and services, including mobile networks, fixed networks, cloud core software, and IP routing. With a global reach spanning over 130 countries, Nokia continues to invest heavily in research and development to maintain its position at the forefront of technological advancements in telecommunications.
