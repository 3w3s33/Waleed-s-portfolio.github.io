Nokia

The visualization of sales data from March 2024 to January 2025 aims to identify the factors behind a significant sales spike in July 2024. The top chart displays the sum of purchase prices over time, revealing fluctuations in sales volume, while the bottom chart shows the count of customer IDs by product model and payment method. The Nokia X10 leads in sales, followed by the Nokia 8356, with cash and direct debit as the most popular payment methods.

Sales were steady from March to May 2024 but spiked notably in July. Possible causes for the spike include promotional activities, seasonal demand trends, or external factors like industry news or economic changes.

To better understand the spike, its recommended to conduct a deeper analysis of specific product models, payment methods, or time periods using tools like Power BI for filtering and drill-down. Incorporating additional data sources, such as product reviews and customer demographics, could provide further insights. Finally, predictive models could help anticipate future sales trends.
