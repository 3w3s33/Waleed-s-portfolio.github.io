Courses page

The "Tech EDU" webpage has a clean and professional layout designed to provide users with an easy-to-navigate experience while exploring various online courses. The page is structured using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with integration of external libraries like Bootstrap for responsiveness and Font Awesome for iconography. It’s optimized for both desktop and mobile users, ensuring accessibility and functionality across devices. The navigation bar at the top includes the Tech EDU logo and a menu with links to the Home, About, Blog, Courses, and Contact pages. The "Courses" link is highlighted to show the user's current location on the website. The menu also includes a hamburger-style button, which when clicked, reveals the navigation links in a mobile-friendly layout. A close button allows users to hide the menu once it’s open, providing a seamless experience on smaller screens. In the "About Home" section, the title "Courses" is prominently displayed, introducing visitors to the course offerings.
Following this is the "Course" section, which showcases a selection of popular courses provided by Tech EDU. Each course is presented with an image, a brief description, an update date, a star rating system for reviews, and the course price. For example, the "JavaScript Beginners Course" and "Angular Beginners Course" are among the highlighted options, with their respective course details and prices. Users can click on any course box, which is designed to be interactive. Upon clicking, they are redirected to a dedicated course page (e.g., course-inner.html) for more detailed information. The courses vary in price, ranging from $69.99 to $129.99, with a consistent rating system that allows prospective students to gauge the course quality based on reviews. The footer section provides additional information and links to related content. The "Top Products" section lists services such as reputation management, power tools, and marketing services, alongside other important offerings. There is also a "Guides" section that includes links to resources such as research and expert advice, helping users find valuable information. Additionally, the "Newsletter" section invites users to subscribe with their email address to receive promotional offers and updates, further engaging visitors and fostering communication. The page ends with a copyright notice and social media icons, encouraging users to follow Tech EDU on platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn.
The footer layout is organized into multiple columns, making it easy to navigate and find relevant sections. For added interactivity, JavaScript is used to manage the navigation menu's behavior. When the "menu-btn" is clicked, the menu items become visible, and when the "menu-close" button is clicked, the menu hides again, providing a user-friendly mobile navigation experience.
