Nokia

The visualization of the Nokia X10 mobile phone focuses on sales data, showing performance over time, by payment type, and product model. The primary question addressed is how Nokia can improve customer retention in a competitive market. Key insights reveal that the Nokia X10 and 8.3 5G have the highest sales, while the Nokia G20 and 7.2 are underperforming. Cash is the most common payment method, followed by credit card, and sales tend to peak in the first quarter of each year.

To improve retention, the visualization can help identify high-value customers by tracking frequent purchasers and their payment preferences. Personalizing offers based on customer history and preferences can also foster loyalty. Additionally, analyzing payment methods may highlight areas for improvement in the transaction process, suggesting the inclusion of more payment options. Monitoring customer satisfaction through integrated data can further aid in retention strategies.
