Items

The physical components used in the project include a door lock, motion sensor, camera module, and a buzzer. The door lock is electronically controlled by the ESP32, allowing users to remotely unlock or lock the door via the Blynk app. The motion sensor detects movement near the door and triggers actions like capturing images or activating the buzzer

The camera module is used to take pictures when requested, providing a visual feed of the entrance area. The buzzer is an alert mechanism that sounds when motion is detected, adding an extra layer of security by notifying users of potential intrusions. These physical items work together with the ESP32 and Blynk app to create a functional, interactive security system.
