Contact page.

This webpage is designed for "Tech EDU," an educational platform, with a primary focus on providing contact information and enabling users to connect with the platform. The layout is modern and responsive, featuring essential sections like navigation, contact forms, location map, and footer, ensuring a smooth user experience across devices. The page starts with a navigation bar at the top, showcasing the "Tech EDU" logo and a navigation menu. The menu items include "Home," "About," "Blog," "Courses," and "Contact," with the "Contact" page highlighted as the active link. The navigation bar is visually appealing and incorporates interactive elements, such as a hamburger-style menu button that toggles the display of the menu on smaller screens. The use of Font Awesome icons, like the "close" icon for closing the menu, adds a polished touch to the design. The main section of the page is dedicated to the contact information. The "Contact Us" section includes a clear heading and an introductory paragraph urging users to fill out a form for assistance. Below the introductory text, multiple contact methods are displayed, such as the physical address of the headquarters, phone numbers, and two support email addresses. Each of these contact methods is accompanied by relevant icons, making it easy for users to understand the type of contact information being provided. There are also social media links, represented by icons for Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn, allowing visitors to connect with Tech EDU on popular platforms. A contact form is also integrated within this section, prompting users to input their name, email, subject, and a message describing how Tech EDU can assist them. Once the user has filled out the form, they can click a "Send message" button to submit their inquiry. The page also includes a map section, which embeds a Google Maps iframe showing the location of Tech EDU's headquarters in Brooklyn, New York. This addition offers visitors a visual representation of the address, further enhancing the contact section by providing geographical context. At the bottom of the page, the footer section features multiple columns, each containing relevant links and information. These include "Top products," "Guides," and a "Newsletter" section where users can subscribe to receive promotional messages and offers. The footer is comprehensive, with quick access to additional resources and updates. It also contains social media icons for Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn, making it easy for users to follow Tech EDU on these platforms. The page is enhanced with a small piece of JavaScript code that controls the display of the navigation menu. When the menu button is clicked, the navigation menu slides into view, and when the "close" icon is clicked, it hides the menu again. This adds a layer of interactivity to the website, making it more dynamic and engaging for visitors.
