Index page.

The "Tech EDU" webpage is designed to attract users by showcasing a variety of educational courses and services, utilizing modern web development techniques such as Bootstrap for responsiveness and Font Awesome icons for visual appeal. The page is structured to be both informative and interactive, offering users an engaging experience. At the top of the page, the navigation bar features the Tech EDU logo and a menu with key links: "Home," "About," "Blog," "Courses," and "Contact." The "Home" link is highlighted as active, indicating the user's current page. For mobile users, the navigation bar includes a hamburger-style menu that can be toggled open or closed, making the site mobile-friendly and easy to navigate. The Hero section, or Home section, is positioned to immediately engage visitors.
It features a bold headline that encourages users to enhance their career with Tech EDU, emphasizing the platformâ€™s global reach and high-quality courses. Accompanying the headline is a brief paragraph outlining the benefits of the platform. Two call-to-action buttons are provided: one for learning more about the platform and another for exploring more courses. These buttons encourage users to take the next step in exploring the offerings. Moving into the Features section, the webpage highlights three key aspects of Tech EDU: scholarships, online Python courses, and global certification. Each of these features is paired with a relevant icon, making the information visually appealing and easy to understand. This section is designed to inform visitors about the unique opportunities available through Tech EDU, including the potential for earning scholarships and gaining globally recognized certifications. The Popular Courses section displays several of the most sought-after courses offered by Tech EDU. For each course, an image is provided, followed by details such as the last update date, course title, ratings in the form of stars, and the course cost.
Courses like "JavaScript Beginners Course," "Angular Beginners Course," and "HTML and CSS Course" are among the options featured. This section allows users to quickly browse available courses and see their key features, encouraging exploration and further engagement. The Registration section is positioned to drive conversions by offering users the opportunity to register for free access to 100 online courses. A countdown timer adds urgency to this offer, showcasing the limited time available to sign up. Below the timer, users are prompted to fill out a registration form with their name, email, and phone number. The inclusion of a prominent call-to-action button provides users with a clear and easy way to sign up. At the bottom of the page, the footer is divided into several columns, each offering different types of information. One column focuses on "Top Products," linking to various services such as reputation management, power tools, and marketing services. Another column provides links to helpful guides, such as research and expert advice. Additionally, a newsletter subscription form invites users to submit their email addresses to receive promotional offers and updates. Social media icons are also included, allowing users to easily connect with Tech EDU on platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn. The page includes interactive JavaScript functionality that enables users to toggle the visibility of the navigation menu when the "menu button" or "close button" is clicked. This feature enhances the usability of the page, especially for mobile visitors.
